Privacy Policy for DenCare Dental Management System

Welcome to DenCare! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our dental management system. By accessing or using DenCare, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Clinic Information: When dental clinics register with DenCare, we collect information such as clinic name, address, contact details, and administrative user details.

1.2. Patient Information: Patients registering on DenCare provide personal details, including name, contact number, email address, and any relevant health information.

1.3. Appointment and Usage Data: We collect data related to appointments, treatment plans, dental charts, and system usage to improve our services.

2. How We Use Information

2.1. Clinic Data: Clinic information is used for account management, system functionality, and communication with the clinic administrators.

2.2. Patient Data: Patient information is utilized for appointment scheduling, record-keeping, and communication with patients regarding their appointments and account status.

2.3. Appointment Notifications: Contact numbers and email addresses may be used to send SMS notifications and emails about upcoming appointments and important system updates.

3. Data Security

3.1. DenCare employs industry-standard security measures to protect the confidentiality and integrity of clinic and patient data.

3.2. Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel and is encrypted during transmission.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1. Clinic information is shared within the DenCare system to facilitate appointments and patient management.

4.2. Patient information is shared with the respective registered clinic for appointment scheduling and record-keeping purposes.

4.3. DenCare does not sell or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. User Control and Access

5.1. Clinics and patients can access, update, or correct their information through the DenCare system.

5.2. Patients have the right to request the removal of their personal information from the system.

6. Data Retention

6.1. We retain clinic and patient data for the duration of the clinic's registration with DenCare or as required by applicable laws.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

7.1. DenCare reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy. Users will be notified of any material changes.
